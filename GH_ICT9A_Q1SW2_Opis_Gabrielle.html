The Characters of Noli Me Tanghere

Images Characters Descriptions
Crisostomo Ibarra

His full name is Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin, and is a Filipino- Spanish man, who was raised in the Philippines and came to Europe to study.
María Clara She is the daughter of a famous and rich man, and her mother died while giving birth to her. María is the epitome of a woman: graceful, delicate, and beautiful.
Padre Damaso Padre Damaso is considered as the antagonist in this book. He is connected to the killing of Don Rafael Ibarra, Crisostomo's father. He is a very shrewd, jelous priest with a large ego.
Kapitan Tiago He is the father of María Clara, and is very soft-hearted. He is always welcoming guests at his house, except for merchants who rip you off.
Tiya Isabel She is Kapitan Tiago's sister, and María Clara's aunt, and teacher. Since the passing of María's mom, Tiya Isabel has always been there for her, and has helped take care of her along with her brother.
Reference: Cruz, E. G.(2018).Noli Me Tanghere ni Jose Rizal. Quezon City: C and E Publishing, Inc.
